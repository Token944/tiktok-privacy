Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025

1. Introduction

n8n TikTok Integration ("we", "our", or "us") operates the TikTok API integration application for automated video publishing. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

Our TikTok application "n8n" collects and processes the following types of information:

  • TikTok Account Information: Basic account information (Open ID, username) with your permission
  • Video Content: Video files and metadata (descriptions, hashtags, settings) that you choose to publish through our application
  • Access Tokens: OAuth access tokens required for API authentication and video publishing
  • Publishing Metadata: Video titles, descriptions, hashtags, privacy settings, and scheduling information
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our application, including API call logs and error messages

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To enable automated video publishing to your TikTok account
  • To process and upload video content to TikTok servers
  • To manage OAuth authentication and maintain secure API connections
  • To improve our service quality and user experience
  • To maintain and improve the functionality of our application
  • To troubleshoot technical issues and provide support

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With TikTok as part of using their Content Posting API services
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • In connection with a business transfer or merger

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes:

  • Encryption of access tokens and sensitive data
  • Secure storage of API credentials
  • Regular security assessments and updates

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We store data only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements

Access tokens are stored securely and can be revoked by you at any time through TikTok's application settings.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out: Opt-out of data collection by disconnecting our application
  • Revoke Access: Revoke our application's access through TikTok Developer Portal or TikTok app settings
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
